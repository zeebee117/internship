Areas of Focus

Anxiety & Panic

Chronic worry, racing thoughts, muscle tension, and difficulty sleeping can quietly take over daily life. Using evidence-based approaches such as CBT and mindfulness practices, we address both the mental patterns and the physiological stress response that keep anxiety in place.

Trauma Therapy (EMDR)

Unresolved trauma—whether from a single event or long-standing patterns—can affect relationships, confidence, and your sense of safety. I integrate EMDR and carefully paced trauma-informed work focused on stabilization, regulation, and meaningful processing.

Burnout & Perfectionism

Many professionals and creatives feel “functional” on the outside while internally exhausted. Therapy becomes a space to reduce high internal pressure, understand perfectionistic patterns, and build sustainable ways of living and working.
